Design Projects

Hospitality environments brought to life end to end — from first concept to manufacturing, shipping and delivery.

01

The Shorely

Fort Lauderdale, FL

The Shorely exudes modern tropical elegance, featuring a two-level open design that maximizes natural light and airflow. The lower-level bar is framed by arches and vibrant botanical wallpaper, creating a cozy yet chic ambiance. Sleek white bar stools complement the neutral-toned counter, while lush greenery enhances the overall vibrancy. Upstairs, minimalist wooden furniture with clean lines and scalloped umbrellas add a coastal, airy vibe — soft teal accents tie the space together, blending modernity with a touch of vintage charm.

02

Tankah Bay Hotel

Riviera Maya, Mexico

The Tankah Bay Hotel seamlessly blends rustic charm with modern elegance. Vibrant patterned tiles, natural wood accents, and woven textures create warmth, while sleek furnishings and minimalist details add contemporary sophistication. The space flows effortlessly, with artisanal touches and functional design enhancing both comfort and style.

03

La Habana

Kimpton Anglers Hotel, South Beach

La Habana is a vibrant oasis of relaxation, blending natural charm with urban sophistication. Rustic wooden tables and chairs are paired with colorful, patterned cushions for a playful yet refined touch. Bold black-and-white striped umbrellas provide shade while adding a graphic element to the lush surroundings of palm trees and blooming purple bougainvillea. The space feels open and inviting — a perfect balance of comfort and elegance for al fresco dining.

04

Nikki Beach Barbados

Port Ferdinand, Barbados

A passionate project with Jada Construction owner Philip Tempro, leading to a collaboration that created a truly remarkable property. Located on the shores of Port Ferdinand, the majestic property boasts a 270-degree view of the ocean, restaurant-side docking, and a built-in pool bar.

05

Nikki Beach Costa Smeralda

Sardinia, Italy

Commissioned by Costa Smeralda Group in concert with Mario Ferrari. The establishment is the apogee of bespoke locations, accessible only by boat and matched only by the unique hues of blue waters distinct to the area. The 90-seat restaurant — adorned in fragrance, privacy, and deep-water docking — provides the ultimate in daytime beach club luxury.

06

One West Las Olas

Fort Lauderdale, FL

Our client wanted to establish a banquette in a nook on the rooftop patio bar at One West Las Olas. It was a pleasure to work with them on the creative side and bring their vision to life.
